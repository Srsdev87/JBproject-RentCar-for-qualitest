Who we are

The Civic Si puts the Si in sick with two sleek yet subdued models: a streamlined sedan and a wing-bearing coupe. The legendary VTEC engines of yore are replaced by a 205-hp turbo 1.5-liter four that syncs solely to a six-speed manual gearbox. Added performance pieces are adaptive dampers, sportier steering, and bigger brakes. Red accents highlight an interior fitted with heated sport seats. Plus, standard treats include rain-sensing wipers and a touchscreen with Apple CarPlay and Android Auto.
