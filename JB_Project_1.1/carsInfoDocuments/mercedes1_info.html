About Mercedes

With as many flavors as a fro-yo shop, the C-class offers luxury and performance in a tidy package. It's available as a sedan, coupe, or cabriolet with standard rear- or optional all-wheel drive. The sedan and coupe come with a 241-hp turbo four or a 362-hp V-6. There’s also a plug-in hybrid version of the sedan with the turbo four, making 275 hp. The cabriolet has the turbo four and a cloth top. Bluetooth, a 7-inch display and forward-collision alert with automatic braking are standard on all.
