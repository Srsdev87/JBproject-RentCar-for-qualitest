About JEEP

With a pavement-melting 707 hp, the Trackhawk is the most powerful SUV in the world. Under the hood is a supercharged 6.2-liter V-8 making 645 lb-ft. That power routes through an eight-speed automatic driving all four wheels all the time. Jeep claims it can hit 60 mph in 3.5 seconds, do the quarter-mile in 11.6 seconds at 116 mph, and hit a top speed of 180 mph. Uconnect with Apple CarPlay and Android Auto are standard, as are adaptive cruise, lane-departure warning, and blind-spot monitoring.
