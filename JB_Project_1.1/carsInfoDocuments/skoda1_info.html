About Skoda

Champion Motors Ltd. , one of the three largest car importers in Israel, is an importer and marketer of "Skoda" cars, along with Volkswagen Group's brands: Volkswagen Commercial and Commercial, Audi and Seat. Champion Motors operates an independent sales, marketing and sales service for Skoda vehicles with 11 showrooms and 22 service centers nationwide.
