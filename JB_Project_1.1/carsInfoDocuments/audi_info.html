About AUDI

The A4 is swift, silent, and sporty—and one of the best in its class. The exterior is understated; its interior handsome and sophisticated with pleasing materials. Handling is stable, though steering feels light at highway speeds. Its turbocharged inline-four makes 252 hp (or 190 in the fuel-saving A4 Ultra) and pairs with a six-speed manual or seven-speed automatic. Front-wheel drive, Apple CarPlay and Android Auto are standard; all-wheel drive and a 19-speaker audio system are optional.
