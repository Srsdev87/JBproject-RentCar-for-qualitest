About KIA

Get behind the wheel of the Stinger and you know it was built by car enthusiasts. The base engine is a 255-hp turbocharged 2.0-liter inline-four; a 365-hp twin-turbo 3.3-liter V-6 is optional. Both models have an eight-speed automatic transmission and either rear- or all-wheel drive. A 7.0-in touchscreen is standard. Available driver aids include adaptive cruise control, automated emergency braking, and lane-keeping assist. An optional 15-speaker Harman/Kardon audio system kicks out the jams.
