ABOUT FIAT

The tiny 500 goes from pipsqueak to bad-ass as the hot-rod Abarth, the heart of which is a raucous 160-hp turbocharged four-cylinder. A five-speed manual is standard, while a six-speed automatic is optional. The exhaust note is addictive, and thanks to a slammed suspension, the Abarth’s handling is as feisty as its styling. There is a coupe or sliding-canvas soft top, though in open-air form, it can get a little pricey. Still, the Abarth is a special car with a ton of charisma.
